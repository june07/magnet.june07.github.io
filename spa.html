Necessity and education are two factors that brought me to write this. A few days ago I saw a need of a user who was attempting to share magnet URI's on a Discourse forum. Investigating further I discovered that there was no ability to insert magnet URI's into the editor. Only Http URI's were allowed. I believe this may also be the case with BBCode in general and other forum editors. Anyway, I needed to be able to redirect from one URI scheme to another (http: to magnet:). I also wanted to learn Angular so the result was this service here. Hope others find it usefull...
